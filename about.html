About Us

iRizq Kitchen is a small business that provides a wide variety of homemade cakes based in Sabak Bernam. This bakery specialises in events such as children's parties and weddings. We are still making cakes at home but with all your support and love, iRizq Kitchen bakery will be officially open soon! We promise to deliver products with the best quality that can be enjoyed by everyone.
