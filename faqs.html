Frequently Asked Questions

Why order with iRizq Kitchen?

Our network of talented bakers is selected to ensure each cake is hand-made freshly, from scratch, to order. As a result, our cakes are still moist and delicious by the time they were delivered to your doorstep.

What makes your products different?

You might have read at least once that we are a scratch bakery, and that fact alone sets us apart from most other bakeries. It improves the taste and quality of the products we make. Our desserts are not the same as other cakes made in factories like those you might purchase from some grocery store bakeries. We never freeze our cakes, though that too is a common practice. Pound cakes are expensive to make, but the unmatched taste is worth every penny. We also have a lighter batter; however, please specify the cake you would like when you place your order.

What if I need to cancel my order?

For fully-paid payment, you will get a refund of 75% of the price or store credit for any cancellations made within 72 hours from the date of the order. For those who paid the deposit on their orders, store credit worth the deposit amount will be given. We do not refund any portion of the purchase price for orders cancelled more than 72 hours from the date of the order.

How much is the deposit when ordering a cake?

We require you to make full payment when the order has been placed. Please note that your order will not be fulfilled if you do not pay for the cake in advance. We accept credit cards over the phone and send the receipt through email for your convenience.

Can I change my order after it has already been placed?

Generally speaking, all orders are considered final once payment has been made and entered into our system, but we still allow customers to change their order with at least an 8-days notice from the day you placed your order. Please note the cost of your cake may increase when you make any customization orders like changing the design, flavors, etc.

What if my cake arrives damaged?

Please do contact us immediately at +60 11-2619 1035 and send across accompanying images orders to @irizq_kitchen if you receive any damaged cake.

How do I leave feedback for an order?

We always like to hear from our customers. Please leave your feedback about us on our feedback page , DM us at @irizq_kitchen , or WhatsApp us with your feedback.
